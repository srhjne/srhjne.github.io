Natural 20 is a fitness tracking app that is themed around Dungeons and Dragons. It allows users to set goals for themselves and track progress against those goals using data from the Google fit API. Thesis: On Global Coronal Magnetic Topology

My thesis was accepted in October 2014. I researched the application of magnetic topology to structures in the solar coronal magnetic field at a global scale using potential magnetic field assumptions.

Magnetic topology divides up otherwise very complex magnetic fields using topological strcutures such as null points (points where the magnetic field is zero) and separators (field lines that connect between null points).
